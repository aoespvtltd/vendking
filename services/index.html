Direct Purchase

Buy the machine outright and manage everything yourself. Best for entrepreneurs who want full control and maximum profits.

  • • Full ownership and control
  • • 100% profit retention
  • • Higher initial investment
  • • Active management required

Collaborator ⭐

Partner with us for a shared investment model. We handle operations while you provide location and share profits.

  • • Shared investment (60-40 split)
  • • Passive income opportunity
  • • We handle operations
  • • Perfect for investors

Rental

Rent machines for events, temporary locations, or to test the market before making a larger commitment.

  • • Low upfront cost
  • • Flexible terms
  • • Perfect for events
  • • Test market viability
