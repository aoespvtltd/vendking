Perfect for Every Location

LphaVend machines are designed to thrive in Nepal's diverse environments

Colleges & Universities

Perfect for busy campus life where students need quick access to snacks, beverages, and essentials between classes.

Ideal Products:

  • • Energy drinks & coffee
  • • Healthy snacks & protein bars
  • • Stationery & supplies
  • • Instant noodles & quick meals

ROI Potential: 20-25% monthly with high foot traffic

Hospitals & Clinics

24/7 operation capability serves patients, visitors, and medical staff around the clock with essential items.

Ideal Products:

  • • Bottled water & juices
  • • Light snacks & crackers
  • • Hygiene products
  • • Pain relief & basic medicines

Advantage: Consistent demand with 24/7 operation

Corporate Offices

Boost employee satisfaction and productivity with convenient access to refreshments and office supplies.

Ideal Products:

  • • Premium coffee & tea
  • • Healthy snacks & fruits
  • • Office supplies
  • • Fresh sandwiches & salads

Benefit: Employee perk that pays for itself

Gyms & Fitness Centers

Cater to health-conscious customers with protein-rich snacks, sports drinks, and fitness accessories.

Ideal Products:

  • • Protein bars & shakes
  • • Sports drinks & electrolytes
  • • Fitness accessories
  • • Energy supplements

Market: Growing fitness culture in Nepal
