Product Selection: What Sells Best in Nepal

Discover the most popular vending machine products in Nepal and how to optimize your product mix for maximum profits — local insights for local success.

LphaVend Team
• February 5, 2026

Choosing the right products is one of the most important decisions in vending. Here's what actually sells best in Nepal in 2026, based on real LphaVend machine data.

Top Categories & Specific Winners

CategoryShare of SalesTop Products
Beverages58–68%Coke, Fanta, Sprite, Water, Sting, Pulse
Snacks18–25%Lays, Kurkure, Wai Wai cup noodles, KitKat
Healthy / Premium8–14%Almonds, Protein bars, Yogurt drinks
Other5–10%Sanitary pads, Masks, Phone chargers (mini)

Seasonal & Location Adjustments

  • Summer → increase cold drinks capacity
  • Exam season → add coffee/tea & energy drinks
  • Colleges → more instant noodles & chips
  • Hospitals → more water & healthy snacks

Pro tip: Keep 2–3 “mystery slots” to test new products every month. Replace slow movers quickly.
