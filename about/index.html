Our Story

Vending King Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a vision to revolutionize the retail landscape in Nepal. We recognized the untapped potential of automated retail solutions in our country and set out to create world-class vending machines that are specifically designed for the Nepalese market.

Our flagship product, LphaVend, represents years of research, development, and understanding of local needs. From power fluctuations to payment preferences, we've built machines that truly work in Nepal's unique environment.

Today, we're proud to be Nepal's leading vending machine manufacturer, creating jobs, supporting local businesses, and contributing to the country's economic modernization.
